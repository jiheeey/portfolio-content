About Me

Email me

Born and raised in Seoul, Korea. Moved to Chicago, IL in 2008, when I was 13 years old. In 2015, I graduated from high school and moved to NYC to study Communication Design at Pratt Institute. I have been practicing art and design since childhood, in fact, I tried almost all domains of arts and designs. So far, I learned and tried: drawing, illustration, painting, sculpture, ceramics, printmaking, jewelery making, metal work, wood work, industrial design, photography, fashion design (sewing), 3D, motion graphic, digital/graphic design, and editorial.

After New York, I moved back to the mid-west to attend the University of Michigan's graduate program in Human Computer Interaction. Currently living in Ann Arbor, MI in a small studio flat near downtown. I enjoy cooking, cleaning, going to farmers market, golfing, swimming, dancing, and attending DJ events (sadly there aren't many in Michigan). I dream to be a DJ someday and produce music as a hobby. If you happen to see this text, you're automatically invited to my DJ party! :) Feel free to send me an email for connection! I'm always down for a good coffee chat.
